# Marketplace Research

## Purpose

This folder contains research and experiments related to the Axie Marketplace.

The goal is to understand how the marketplace values different Axie characteristics and to develop data-driven purchasing and selling strategies.

Unlike daily logs, the documents in this folder capture reusable knowledge and long-term research findings.

---

## Planned Research

### 1. Marketplace Pricing Study

Objective

Determine how much the market values:

- Level
- Breed Count
- Class
- Evolution
- Collectible Status
- Parts
- Ascension
- Other characteristics

---

### 2. Embedded Value Strategy

Investigate whether purchasing Axies with existing investments (such as ascension levels) provides better value than purchasing floor-priced Axies.

---

### 3. Release Economics

Research the relationship between:

- Purchase price
- Breed count
- Release materials
- Net profitability

---

### 4. Marketplace Flip Strategy

Analyze profitable buy-and-sell opportunities.

Metrics include:

- Holding period
- ROI
- Profit
- Win rate

---

### 5. Marketplace Intelligence

Future AI module that estimates:

- Fair Value
- Discount
- Embedded Value
- Buy / Hold / Sell recommendation

---

## Design Principle

Research documents answer **why**.

Daily logs record **what happened**.

Blockchain data records **how it happened**.
