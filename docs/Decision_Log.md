# Decision Log

## Purpose

This document records important architectural and technical decisions made during the development of AxieOS.

## This document answers

- What technical decision was made?
- Why was it made?
- What alternatives were considered?
- What impact does the decision have?

## Audience

- Project Owner
- Contributors
- Future AI assistants
- Future developers

## Update Rules

- Record only significant architectural or technical decisions.
- Never delete previous decisions.
- If a decision changes, create a new entry instead of modifying history.
- Reference related Engineering Journal entries when appropriate.
